La Bandera Dominicana

La Bandera

La Bandera is Dominican Republic's main lunch meal. They call it "The Flag", because it represents Dominicans's cousine.

La Bandera consists of white rice, chicken stew and red beans, served with tasty salad and avocado!

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Chicken
  • Red Beans
  • Cabbage
  • Cucumber
  • Tomatoes
  • Avocado

Cooking Steps

  1. Cook white rice
  2. Boil red beans and season
  3. Cook chicken with seasoning
  4. Make salad
  5. Cut slices of avocado
  6. Enjoy!
